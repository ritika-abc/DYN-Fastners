About us

We are DYN Fastners

Innovation & Engineering Excellence

Uncompromised Product Quality

Established with a vision to strengthen industries worldwide, DYN Fastners is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of high-quality industrial fasteners. Our wide range includes bolts, nuts, screws, washers, rivets, U-bolts, and more — all engineered to deliver superior performance and reliability.

Serving clients across automotive, construction, aerospace, and heavy machinery sectors, we are committed to precision, quality, and customer satisfaction in every product we deliver.
